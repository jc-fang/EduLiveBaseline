Educational live stream: Annotating while watching educational live stream



Please read the following information carefully before proceeding.
This study needs you to learn through an educational live stream. You are eligible for the study if you do not have a disability of hearing, reading or judging. You need to be a student and at least 18 years old to attend the study. You also need to be proficient in English. If you have vision, hearing or judgment impairment, please don't move forward.

What will you do?
In this task, you will (1) learn through the live stream lecture and make annotations (highlights/questions) based on the real-time transcipt, and afterwards (2) fill out the survey. The task will last about 60 minutes. You will receive a $10 payment through Prolific after completing the task.
Please follow the instructions closely at all times when doing this task. Your assignment may be rejected if we detect that you did not pay attention when doing this task or we identify obvious evidence that you were not taking the task seriously.
Please do not close the webpage once you start until you complete the whole task (i.e. after you finish the survey and see the 'thank you' page). After you see the 'thank you' page, make sure to click on the provided link and return to the Prolific webpage to get your payment.
